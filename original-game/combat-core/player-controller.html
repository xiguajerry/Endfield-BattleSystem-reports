    字段分组¶

    PlayerController 不是只维护 mainCharacter，它同时是本地玩家控制输入的聚合点。 核心字段可以按职责分组:

    分组 字段
    当前主控 mainCharacter
    走跑/摇杆 m_inSprint, m_lastSprintInput, m_sprintNoInputTime, m_joystickState
    移动坐标/轴限制 m_fixMoveCoordinate, m_fixedMoveForward, m_fixedMoveRight, m_currentLimitInputAxisHandle
    全局步态限制 m_globalMoveGaitLimitHandleCounter, m_globalMoveGaitLimitDict
    输入屏蔽 m_blockPlayerInputHashSet
    动作使能 m_currentHandleId, m_playerActionEnableMask, m_playerActionEnableHandle
    普攻缓存 m_canKeepAttackingTimer, m_keepAttackingTimer, touch keep attack timers
    物理/地面状态 m_lastFloor, m_lastFloorComponentIndex, m_lastMainCharInAir, m_inAirForbidHandle
    主控 tag m_tagContainer, m_addedTagHandle
